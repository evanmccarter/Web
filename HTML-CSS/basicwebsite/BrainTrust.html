Our Services

Mission Operations and Deployment

BrainTrust engineers take mission critical systems from concept to field in support of national requirements.  We specialize in building systems that work in rugged environments and produce results.  Our field-hardened veterans are ready to go where the mission demands.  Anytime.  Anywhere.

Database Design and Optimization

BrainTrust has experience with Cloud Data storage (e.g. Accumulo), NoSQL data stores (e.g. MongoDB) and traditional relational databases (e.g. Oracle, mysql).   Our experts can structure the data for maximum utility, performance, and security.  Data is the core element of any Information Technology system.

System Engineering

System Engineering is a core strength at BrainTrust.  We know what it takes to design and integrate complex systems to meet time-sensitive and mission-critical requirements.  We draw from our full team of technologists to produce best of breed solutions.  BrainTrust is a great place to learn and grow in System Engineering.

Cloud Computing

Cloud Computing is a revolutionary computing paradigm that allows commodity hardware to be used in parallel to provide super computer level resources for modest costs.  BrainTrust leads the way in Cloud Computing – developing core libraries, data formats, and complex Cloud Analytics.

Data Analysis and Metadata Modeling

BrainTrust analysts and data scientists are experts at finding the needle in the haystack.  They structure data for efficient digest by complex algorithms and allow massive data stores to be turned into sources of valuable information.  BrainTrust can help with ingest, filtering, enriching, selecting, modeling, and interpreting data.

Software Design and Development

Custom software development is a core strength at BrainTrust.  Our seasoned software developers enjoy a challenge and will bring the right tools for the job.  We are well versed in industry best practices and cover all major languages and frameworks.  We use version control and agile methodologies to mitigate risk and show progress status are regular intervals.
